Help users to

Make a declaration

Help users to tell you that they have understood or agree to something before they submit an application or an online form. This is known as a ‘declaration’.

Requirements

    As a form designer, I need the ability to:

    • add a declaration to the bottom of 'Check your answer pages'
    • create a seperate page for users to accept a declaration
    • add text to the declaration that can be formatted using HTML markup e.g., bullet lists and headings

Main changes
What we've done Why we did it
Removed characted limit to declaration text

In the case of the XL Bully form, the policy team insisted on a large declaration to users. The GOV.UK Form builder has a 2000 character limit.

This resulted in having an extra long 'check your answers' page as there can be up to 40 questions and the declaration is added underneath.

Ideally for large forms we'd have declaration page that's shown either before or after the check your answers page.

Added the ability to change checkbox label titles when creating the declaration component pattern. To avoid conflicts with page button labels and allows form designers to adapt content to specific types of declarations
